HAHA KITCHEN

Welcome to Haha Kitchen, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Our name reflects our philosophy - bringing happiness and laughter to every dining experience. Our skilled chefs craft each dish with the finest ingredients, combining traditional techniques with contemporary flair, creating memorable moments around exceptional food.

Signature Tasting

品尝我们匠心独运的美食佳肴，让福建菜的地道灵魂与南方菜的经典风味完美融合。每一道菜都在讲述关于工艺与创新的故事，让您的每一次用餐，都成为一场触动味蕾与记忆的小型旅程。

Crispy Pork Ribs with Potato Wadges in House Made Dressing

A traditional Fuzhou delicacy. Selected pork ribs are fried until crispy on the outside and tender inside, then served with our secret garlic-infused sweet and sour sauce. The perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors is enhanced by a subtle hint of wine and garlic, creating an irresistible taste that keeps you coming back for more.

Braised Free Range Chicken with Longjing Tea

A signature must-try dish. We use premium free-range chicken, carefully marinated and roasted with Longjing tea. The golden-brown skin is crispy while the meat remains tender and juicy. The elegant tea aroma perfectly balances the richness, delivering a delightful taste that is flavorful yet not greasy.

Radish and Beef Offal Claypot

A classic Cantonese favorite. Premium beef offal and sweet white radish are slow-cooked together in a claypot for hours. The offal becomes tender and flavorful, while the radish absorbs the rich essence of the savory broth. Every bite offers a warm and satisfying comfort.
